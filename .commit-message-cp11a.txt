checkpoint 11a — the anticipator pass, wave A (passive temporal intelligence)

Aiteall holds time on behalf of users who cannot reliably hold it in
their head. CP11a is the speaks-when-spoken-to half — calibrate the
temporal model first, then layer proactive surfaces in CP11b.

11a.1 buildWeekAheadContext() in src/services/calendar.ts:
- Pulls 7 days of calendar events (filters out [Aiteall]-prefixed ones
  to avoid double-counting our own skeleton-projected events)
- Overlays per-day skeleton blocks from the weekly template
- Per-day committed + habitual minutes; heaviness label
  (open/light/moderate/busy/packed); rough open-hours estimate
- Output sections: TODAY (detailed), TOMORROW (detailed), REST OF
  THE WEEK (compact one-liners), DEADLINE TASKS within window,
  REMAINING TODAY with the-one called out, and CONFIDENCE NOTES
  teaching the model the three tiers (committed/habitual/claimed)
- Local-time YYYY-MM-DD via ymd() — no toISOString() that would
  shift TZ and bin events into the wrong day
